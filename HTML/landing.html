Welcome to The Linguart Project

Type <anything> you want...

The internet is full of image files. All are connected or disconnected. Enter The Linguart Project to create new meanings out of your input.

In the space provided write anything you want. A story. A confession. A poem... There are no limits. No censorship. After submitting, all the words will be converted to images. A photo-story, Image poetry. Images will generate new meanings, new concepts, new stories. Others can resignify your story by submitting their own interpretations.

Share your stories to the world, have others interpret your confession.

...And see your text come to <life> in images

Help spread the <word> of this experiment

The purpose of this experiment is to push the user to create new meanings by the connection of various images that result from the different words in the search box. One of the main challenges would be to have a stranger interpret and possible generate a new meaning from the images that are being shown.

Furthermore, the project aims to interpret and reinterpret the images, generating a piece of digital art that will encompass all of the words/stories in a single image. Abstraction and interpretation will be at play

It is therefore not the content of the images in a film which is important, but their combination.
